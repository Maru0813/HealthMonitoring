E-KONSULTA

An electronic consultation in this context involves users to input personal information, blood pressure, and pulse rate data, after which they receive automated feedback. This digital interaction allows individuals to assess and monitor their health metrics conveniently. The system generates insights, recommendations, or educational information based on the provided health data, empowering users to make informed decisions about their well-being. It streamlines the process of self-assessment and promotes health awareness through user-friendly electronic interfaces.

HOW TO CONSULT?

This is only applicable for patients who are NOT requiring urgent medical care. E-Konsulta is for health monitoring (such as BMI, BP, & Pulse Rate) only.
